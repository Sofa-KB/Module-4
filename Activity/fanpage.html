Bio


Gojira are a French heavy metal band from Bayonne.

Originally formed as Godzilla in 1996, they changed their name to Gojira in 2001.

Their lyrics focus on life, ecology, and spiritual themes.

Genres that they have been associated with over their career include death metal,

progressive metal, thrash metal and groove metal.
